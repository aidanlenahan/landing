Terms of Service for Aidan Lenahan Dog Walking services

1. Service Description:

The dog walker will not only walk the dog(s), but if requested, will feed, water, play, with the dog(s). Any other services needed shall be requested via email or text.



2. Pricing and Payment:

Rates for each walk/period will be discussed beforehand, and may depend on the following:
- Number of dogs
- Amount of time per period
- Day of week
- Holiday status
- Time of year
(It may be inconvenient or busy for the dog walker at the time of request)



3. Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy:

The customer may cancel a walk at any period of time, however it is requested that it is done within a day in advance.



Liability and Insurance:

The walker will take every precaution to ensure the safety of the dog(s). However, the walker is not liable for any injuries, illnesses, or incidents that may occur during the services.
